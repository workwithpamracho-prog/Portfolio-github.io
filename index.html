Dedication to Detail

Hi, I'm Frances Pamela — a results-driven Executive Virtual Assistant and Operations Specialist based in the heart of Cebu City. With over six years of experience spanning multiple industries and international clients, I bring a rare blend of executive-level support, operational clarity, and creative execution to every engagement.

My journey has taken me from the fast-paced world of travel advisory at Expedia Chase, where I mastered high-volume client management and complex itinerary curation, to executive assistance for film production companies in California, where no detail is too small and every deadline is non-negotiable.

I've supported operations for an Italian-based SaaS company, delivered communication and brand strategies for a US-based LLC, and provided frontline customer support and CRM management for telecom and tech brands. Along the way, I've evolved into a content creator, brand storyteller, and digital marketer who helps businesses grow their presence with purpose.

What sets me apart? I don't just complete tasks — I anticipate needs, streamline systems, and protect your time so you can focus on what only you can do.
